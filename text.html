Sometimes it is useful to create your own vocabulary list from a foreign text. This tool helps you prepare text before building a personal dictionary. You can:
  1. Remove extra spaces (tabs, line breaks, double spaces)
  2. Remove punctuation, brackets and special characters
  3. Convert text to lowercase
  4. Remove duplicate words and numbers
  5. Sort words alphabetically (A→Z or Z→A)
Recommended usage:
  • Paste your text into the input field.
  • Click the buttons to apply the desired operations step by step.
  • After processing, copy the cleaned text.
  • The final result can be pasted into a spreadsheet, where you can later add translations, notes or word frequency.
