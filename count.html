[?]
This tool analyses your text and provides useful statistics. You can:
  1. Check the text length (number of characters)
  2. Count the number of words
  3. Count the number of unique words
  4. View the frequency of each word in the text
Recommended usage:
  • Paste your text into the input field.
  • Select one of the available operations: text length, words count, unique words, or words frequency.
  • The result appears in the corresponding output field.
  • You can copy the processed data for further use.
